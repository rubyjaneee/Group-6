Company History

Halo-Halo de Frutas were established by Group 6 during summer season. Summer season means hot weather.Many people are craving for cold dessert that will refreshingly satisfy themselves. This college student grab the opportunity to release a dessert that will compliment the weather, and at the same time he wanted to give his fellow filipinos a healthy and traditional one that will make their customers feel the taste of being a Filipino. He released a traditional halo-halo dessert with many varieties and they also offer other refreshing dessert.

02

Vision

To see our customers getting satisfied and enjoys our dessert. To see them patronizing our store and also helps their relationships bond together through our unique and special Halo-Halo.

03

Mission

To serve customer with a refreshing Halo-Halo with fresh natural fruits and to satisfy customer with a drink that will beat the heat of this summer. Also to ensure that every customer who purchases our product will be satisfied.
